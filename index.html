Here are the Rules

This game of Ping Pong will take your number, count up to that number from 1 with some fun exceptions:

      1. Numbers divisible by 3 will be replaced with Ping!
      2. Numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced with Pong!
      3. Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 will be replaced with Ping Pong!
