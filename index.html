Peanut Butter & Jelly!

Welcome to a game of Ping Pong!

(Revamped as Peanut Butter & Jelly)

Here are the Rules:

This game of Ping Pong will take your number, count up to that number from 1 with some fun exceptions:

      1. Numbers divisible by 3 will be replaced with Peanut Butter!
      2. Numbers divisible by 5 will be replaced with Jelly!
      3. Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 will be replaced with Peanut Butter & Jelly! Yum!
